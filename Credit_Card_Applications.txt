Base de datos de peticiones de tarjeta de credito

CustomerID
A1 Common Columns (Anonymized Version)
A2 Gender: (Nominal) Gender of the applicant.
A3 Age: (Continuous) Age of the applicant.
A4 Debt: (Continuous) Outstanding debt.
A5 Married: (Nominal) Marital status.
A6 BankCustomer: (Nominal) Whether they are a bank customer.
A7 EducationLevel: (Ordinal) Highest level of education.
A8 Ethnicity: (Nominal) Ethnicity of the applicant.
A9 YearsEmployed: (Continuous) Number of years employed.
A10 PriorDefault: (Nominal) Whether they have a prior default.
A11 Employed: (Nominal) Whether they are employed.
A12 CreditScore: (Continuous) Credit history score.
A13 DriversLicense: (Nominal) Whether they hold a driver's license.
A14 Citizen: (Nominal) Citizenship status.
A15 ZipCode: (Continuous) Zip code of the applicant.
A16 Income: (Continuous) Annual income.
A17 ApprovalStatus: (Target Variable) Whether the app was approved (+ or -)
