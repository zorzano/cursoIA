Base de datos de peticiones de tarjeta de credito

Common Columns (Anonymized Version)
Gender: (Nominal) Gender of the applicant.
Age: (Continuous) Age of the applicant.
Debt: (Continuous) Outstanding debt.
Married: (Nominal) Marital status.
BankCustomer: (Nominal) Whether they are a bank customer.
EducationLevel: (Ordinal) Highest level of education.
Ethnicity: (Nominal) Ethnicity of the applicant.
YearsEmployed: (Continuous) Number of years employed.
PriorDefault: (Nominal) Whether they have a prior default.
Employed: (Nominal) Whether they are employed.
CreditScore: (Continuous) Credit history score.
DriversLicense: (Nominal) Whether they hold a driver's license.
Citizen: (Nominal) Citizenship status.
ZipCode: (Continuous) Zip code of the applicant.
Income: (Continuous) Annual income.
ApprovalStatus: (Target Variable) Whether the app was approved (+ or -)
